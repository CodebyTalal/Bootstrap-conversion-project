Kitchen Color Trends for 2023: Mixing and Matching Patterns and Solids

Lucy Bond is an interior designer who started her career in New Zealand, working for large architectural firms. We chatted to her about design and starting her own studio.

Introduction

Are you ready to give your kitchen a fresh and modern update for 2023? One trend that we're loving for the new year is the idea of mixing and matching patterns and solids in your kitchen color scheme. This allows you to create a unique and eye-catching look that's sure to make a statement. To get started with this trend, consider choosing a bold and vibrant solid color for your walls or cabinets. You can then add pops of pattern through your backsplash, countertops, or even your appliances. For example, if you choose a deep red for your walls, you could pair it with a patterned backsplash in shades of blue and white. Or, if you prefer a more neutral look, you could choose a warm beige for your cabinets and add a patterned countertop in shades of grey and black. One thing to keep in mind when mixing and matching patterns and solids is to choose patterns and colors that complement each other. You don't want to create a cluttered or overwhelming look, so try to stick to a cohesive color palette and choose patterns that have a similar style or theme. Another option is to mix and match within the same color family. For example, you could choose a deep blue for your walls and then add accents in shades of light blue and navy. This creates a cohesive look that's still visually interesting. Overall, the key to this trend is to have fun and be creative. Don't be afraid to mix and match patterns and solids to create a kitchen that's uniquely yours. With the right color combination, you can create a kitchen that's both stylish and functional, and that you'll love spending time in for years to come.
